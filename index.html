About

I am an extremely creative, solution-oriented software developer with a passion for helping others. Before pursuing a career in development, I worked in Education and Healthcare. During my last year of working in Healthcare, I became interested in software development.

My interest would eventually lead me to Nashville Software School where I participated in a Web Development Jumpstart course. I immediately recognized that software development was the perfect combination of creative and strategic problem solving, and I was hooked! In July of 2019, I officially began my career transition with Nashville Software School's full-stack web development boot camp.

I love development because it allows me to be my natural self - curious, creative, and thoughtful. I look forward to starting my career as a developer.
